Scrambled Eggs üç≥

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil or butter

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil or butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour in the eggs and cook, stirring gently until they are softly scrambled.
  4. Serve warm with a slice of whole-grain bread.

Grilled Chicken Breast üçó

Ingredients:

  • 150g chicken breast
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken breast in soy sauce, salt, pepper, and a drizzle of olive oil for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Grill the chicken for about 6-7 minutes on each side or until cooked through.
  4. Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.

Cabbage Salad ü•ó

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon olive oil, lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine shredded cabbage, cucumber, and onion.
  2. Drizzle with soy sauce and optional olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Toss well and serve chilled.

Chicken Stir-Fry üç≤

Ingredients:

  • 150g grilled chicken, sliced
  • 1 cup cabbage, sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add sliced onion and stir until softened.
  3. Add cabbage and cucumber, stir-frying for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add the grilled chicken and soy sauce, stirring until heated through.
  5. Serve warm.

Boiled Eggs üç≥

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs

Instructions:

  1. Place the eggs in a saucepan and cover with water.
  2. Bring to a boil over medium heat.
  3. Once boiling, cover the pan and remove it from heat. Let sit for 9-12 minutes.
  4. Remove the eggs and place them in cold water to cool before peeling.

Chicken Salad ü•ó

Ingredients:

  • 150g grilled chicken, sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 onion, diced
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Optional: Olive oil, lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Combine the grilled chicken, cucumber, onion, and cabbage in a bowl.
  2. Add soy sauce and optional olive oil and lemon juice.
  3. Toss well and serve cold.

Chicken Curry üçõ

Ingredients:

  • 150g chicken, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1-2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Spices (optional: turmeric, cumin, coriander)
  • 1 cup water

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, saut√© chopped onions until golden.
  2. Add cubed chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in soy sauce and any spices you like.
  4. Pour in water and simmer until chicken is cooked through and tender (about 15-20 minutes).
  5. Serve with rice.

Baked Chicken üçó

Ingredients:

  • 150g chicken breast
  • Salt, pepper, and any herbs/spices (e.g., rosemary, thyme)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. Rub chicken with olive oil, salt, pepper, and herbs.
  3. Place in a baking dish and bake for 25-30 minutes or until cooked through.
  4. Let it rest before slicing.

Fried Rice üçö

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, diced
  • 1/4 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and saut√© until soft.
  3. Add cabbage and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in the cooked rice and diced chicken, and add soy sauce.
  5. Mix well and cook for another 5 minutes. Serve hot.

Chicken Wrap üåØ

Ingredients:

  • 150g grilled chicken, sliced
  • 1 chapati
  • 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 onion, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce

Instructions:

  1. Lay the chapati flat and add the shredded cabbage, cucumber, and onion.
  2. Add the sliced grilled chicken on top.
  3. Drizzle with soy sauce, then roll the chapati tightly into a wrap.
  4. Slice in half and serve.
